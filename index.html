About Spin the Bat Trivia

Welcome to Spin the Bat Trivia, your ultimate destination for testing your Major League Baseball knowledge through interactive trivia questions! Whether you're a lifelong fan who lives for the crack of the bat or a newcomer curious about America's pastime, our site offers a unique way to dive into baseball's most fascinating moments. Spin the bat over home plate to get a trivia question, then select the correct answer—complete with a fun fact, story, and reliable source—spanning legendary players, unforgettable games, and quirky statistics.

Spin the Bat Trivia was born from a passion for the game and a desire to challenge fans worldwide with its hidden gems. Our carefully curated trivia collection quizzes you on the heroes of the diamond, from Jackie Robinson's groundbreaking debut to Cal Ripken Jr.'s ironman streak, while uncovering lesser-known tales—like the marathon 25-inning game that tested endurance like no other. Every spin delivers a question, followed by an answer you can trust, blending education with entertainment to keep you coming back for more.

Perfect for fans of all ages, Spin the Bat Trivia lets you test your knowledge, stump your friends, or enjoy a moment of baseball nostalgia. Our interactive design—featuring a spinning bat and home plate—turns trivia into a game that's more than just facts. Share your favorite questions and answers on Twitter with a single click and join a community of enthusiasts celebrating MLB's past and present. Step up to the plate, spin the bat, and let Spin the Bat Trivia bring the excitement of baseball history straight to your screen!
